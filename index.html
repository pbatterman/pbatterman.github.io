- I am a Sophomore studying at the University of Pennsylvania (expected graduation May 2017).

- I am a candidate for a Bachelors of Science in Engineering in Digital Media Design (Essentially Computer Science + Design).

What is Digital Media Design, you may ask? The name doesn't do it justice, but it's a computer science degree at the University of Pennsylvania School of Engineering and Applied Science with an emphasis on computer graphics and design. I have the same foundations as any Computer Science major, such as algorithms, data structures, and computing systems. Additionally, a good portion of my coursework is dedicated to studying the arts. Some of my more recent interests are mechanical design, advertising, and the oxford comma.


I have a great passion for the arts, primarily graphic design, but also in theatrical design, painting, drawing, photography, and carpentry. I do a lot of freelance graphic design work around campus, and am currently involved with a number of Penn's student theater companies.


I am the Public Relations chair for Theta Tau, Penn's premier co-educational professional engineering fraternity.

I play a variety of instruments, including, but not limited to: guitar, piano, and drums. I can also raise each eyebrow independently of the other, and can wiggle my right ear.
