More about me

I am a junior at the University of Pennsylvania School of Engineering and Applied Science majoring in Digital Media Design. What is Digital Media Design, you may ask? It's a computer science degree with an emphasis on computer graphics and design. I have the same foundation as a Computer Science major, such as algorithms, data structures, and computing systems. The rest of my coursework is dedicated to studying art and design. Some of my more recent interests are mechanical design and advertising. For relevant coursework, please check out my resume.


I have a great passion for the arts, primarily graphic design, but also in theatrical design, painting, drawing, photography, and carpentry. I do a lot of freelance graphic design work around campus, and am currently involved several of Penn's student theater companies. For the highlights of my artistic endeavors, please see my portfolio.


I am the Public Relations chair for Theta Tau, Penn's premier co-educational professional engineering fraternity.
