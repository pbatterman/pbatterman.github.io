More about me

I am a sophomore at the University of Pennsylvania studying Digital Media Design. What is Digital Media Design, you may ask? The name doesn't do it justice, but it's a computer science degree at the University of Pennsylvania School of Engineering and Applied Science with an emphasis on computer graphics and design. I have the same foundations as any Computer Science major, such as algorithms, data structures, and computing systems. Additionally, a good portion of my coursework is dedicated to studying the arts. Some of my more recent interests are mechanical design, advertising, and the oxford comma. For relevant coursework, please check out my resume.
