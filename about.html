Who am I?


I am a sophomore at the University of Pennsylvania studying Digital Media Design. What is Digital Media Design, you may ask? It's different for every single student; it's an amalgamation of Computer Science, Design, and miscellaneous electives. My favorite explanation of Digital Media Design is as follows:

We may not necessarily the best programmers or the best artists, but of the programmers, we're the best artists, and of the artists, we're the best programmers.

(Realistically, it's a computer science degree with an emphasis on computer graphics and design). For relevant coursework, please check out my resume.



As to the shoes on my hands:

I have a great passion for the arts, primarily graphic design, but also in theatrical design, painting, drawing, photography, and carpentry. I do a lot of freelance graphic design work around campus, and am currently involved with a number of Penn's student theater companies. For the highlights of my artistic endeavors, please see my portfolio.
